Transpose Notes

As a musician rooted in Nepali culture and the instruments from the childhood. I often dreamt of creating something personal to music. I found it as quite a hassle to save multiple notations and its mostly time consuming to transpose the note from one to another.

Transpose Notes allows a user to view the notation in devnagiri form and also transpose the notes from one scale to another. The notation is in bhatkhande style.
